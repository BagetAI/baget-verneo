## 1. Federal Subsidies: National Schemes & Landlord Constraints

The French state provides robust financial mechanisms for energy transition under France Renov', but each comes with critical legal gating rules regarding property usage.

### MaPrimeRenov' Bailleurs (Individual Landlord Track)
This is an individual grant paid directly to landlords renting out properties.

*   **Eligibility Constraints:** Limited to a maximum of 3 rented properties per landlord. The landlord must formally commit to renting the property as a **primary residence** (tenant occupying at least 8 months per year) for a minimum duration of **6 years** [1.2.8]. 
*   **Strategic Conflict:** This grant is **incompatible** with Verneo's premium high-season short-term tourist rental model, as tourist use violates the 6-year primary residence lease covenant.
*   **Best Use Case:** Exclusively target this for units that will be placed under continuous long-term leases, or where the landlord is willing to forego short-term seasonal yields in favor of high upfront state aid.

### MaPrimeRenov' Copropriete (Collective Building Track)
This grant is voted by the co-ownership assembly (Syndic) and applies to global works on the building envelope (roof insulation, external wall insulation, collective heating) [1.2.6].

*   **Eligibility Constraints:** The building must be registered in the national copropriete registry, be at least 15 years old, and have at least 75% of lots occupied as primary residences [1.2.6]. The planned works must achieve a minimum of **35% energy gain** (verified by a pre-project thermal audit) [1.2.6, 3.3.5].
*   **Strategic Benefit:** Paid directly to the Syndicat de Copropriete. The subsidy is distributed to individual co-owners based on their share of the building (tantiemes) [3.3.1]. Crucially, **there are no individual rental covenants or short-term rental bans attached to this collective grant**, making it fully compatible with Verneo's hybrid short-term model.
*   **2026 Funding Rates:**
    *   **30% subsidy rate** of the total cost of works for a 35% to 49% energy gain (capped at a work ceiling of EUR 25,000 per unit) [3.3.5].
    *   **45% subsidy rate** of the total cost of works for a 50%+ energy gain (capped at a work ceiling of EUR 25,000 per unit) [3.3.5].
    *   **Passoire Thermique Bonus:** An additional **10% bonification** is unlocked if the property successfully transitions out of F or G status to at least an E rating [3.3.5].

### Certificats d'Economies d'Energie (CEE Premiums)
Private sector energy savings certificates funded by major energy suppliers (EDF, TotalEnergies) under the "polluter pays" mandate [1.4.3, 3.4.3].

*   **Eligibility Constraints:** Available for both primary and secondary residences [1.4.5]. No rental covenants or tenant duration restrictions apply [1.4.8]. Works must be executed by RGE-certified contractors [1.3.4, 3.4.2].
*   **Strategic Benefit:** High compatibility with Verneo's short-term and hybrid models. These can be claimed for "single-action" upgrades (e.g., window retrofitting, double glazing, smart radiators) [1.3.5, 3.4.9].

## 3. Local Municipal Subsidies: Paris & Lyon

Both target metropolises offer hyper-localized, supplementary funding pools that can be stacked with national CEE and MaPrimeRenov' Copropriete grants.

### Paris: Eco-Renovons Paris +
A municipal fund of over EUR 51 million deployed by the Mairie de Paris to accelerate the thermal upgrade of residential buildings [2.1.1].

*   **The Mechanism:** Personalized technical guidance coupled with direct municipal grants. It operates at the copropriete level (including single-ownership buildings) [1.3.1, 2.1.1].
*   **Eligibility Rules:** The co-ownership must undergo an environmental diagnostic, and works must target comprehensive thermal improvements.
*   **Subsidy Structure:**
    *   Covers up to **15% to 30%** of the remainder of work costs after national ANAH grants are applied.
    *   Specific top-up grants of **EUR 1,000 to EUR 3,000** per lot are allocated for low-income co-owners.
    *   Includes a specialized focus on bio-sourced insulation materials (wood fiber, hemp lining), which fits the strict Architecte des Batiments de France (ABF) constraints in historic Parisian neighborhoods.

### Lyon: Eco-Renov' Metropole de Lyon
A regional program designed for energy retrofits across the 59 municipalities of the Lyon Metropolitan Area [3.2.2].

*   **The Mechanism:** Accessed through the CoachCopro Lyon platform [3.2.4]. This program is renowned for being highly inclusive and accessible to both owner-occupiers and landlords [1.3.4, 3.2.4].
*   **Eligibility Rules:** The building must achieve at least a **35% energy savings gain** through comprehensive renovations. No restrictive rental caps are imposed on individual landlords when accessing collective building-level funding.
*   **Subsidy Structure:**
    *   Provides up to **EUR 18,650** in direct metropolitan subsidies per dwelling unit, depending on the post-renovation energy performance bracket, regardless of household income [1.3.4].
    *   An additional eco-bonus is granted for using sustainable, non-fossil fuel heating systems (e.g., connecting to Lyon's central heat network or installing thermodynamic systems) [1.4.5, 3.2.7].

### The "No-Covenant" Financial Stack (Best for Short-Term / Hybrid)
For clients utilizing Verneo's premium co-hosting model (Airbnb + low-season Bail Mobilite), we must bypass MaPrimeRenov' Bailleurs to protect booking flexibility. The optimal financial stack for a standard 35 sq. m. F/G-rated studio retrofit is structured below:

1.  **Reduce Upfront Cost via TVA 5.5%:** Automatically apply the reduced rate on all insulation materials and RGE labor, saving 14.5% compared to standard construction works [1.1.2, 3.4.9].
2.  **Claim CEE Single-Action Premiums:** Partner with energy suppliers to secure between **EUR 800 and EUR 2,000** for window double glazing (Tier C) or thermodynamic water heating (Tier B) [1.3.5, 3.4.9].
3.  **Leverage Building-Level MaPrimeRenov' Copropriete:** If the building's general assembly votes for external insulation or roof upgrades, ensure the owner claims their share (covering **30% to 45%** of common-area costs plus the EUR 1,500/lot passoire bonus) without signing any personal rental duration agreements [3.3.1, 3.3.5].
4.  **Bail Mobilite Tax Write-offs:** In the hybrid model, the cost of the remaining renovation works (the "reste a charge") can be deducted as an expense or amortized under the **LMNP (Loueur en Meuble Non Professionnel)** real-regime tax scheme [1.1.2], completely wiping out the landlord's rental tax liabilities for up to 10 years.
